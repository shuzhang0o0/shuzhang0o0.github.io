Research interests

My research interests include combinatorics and graph theory. To be specific, I am interested in the following topics.

  1. Ramsey theory
  2. Turán type problems
  3. Graph coloring problems
  4. Algebraic combinatorics

As an undergraduate, I am also learning and exploring new topics. Due to my research training experience, I am also interested in the interdisciplinary topics in combinatorics and optimization. One may refer to this page for more information on this field. Interestingly, some graph coloring problems, like the classical four-color problem, can also be viewed as combinatorial optimization problems.

Research experiences and publications
  • Clocks are e-positive, Discrete Mathematics 349, (2026), https://doi.org/10.1016/j.disc.2025.114723.
    The work in this paper was mainly completed in PACE 2024, an REU project focusing on algebraic combinatorics, held by the Beijing International Center for Mathematical Research and Peking University. This is joint work with Luan Chen, and our mentor is David Guoliang Wang. Here is a photo of our group (from left to right: Luan Chen, me, David Guoliang Wang). In this project, our group focused on the positivity of chromatic symmetric functions and showed the $e$-positivity of a special class of theta graphs using the idea of the composition method introduced by our mentor in the paper A composition method for neat formulas of chromatic symmetric functions.
  • Model-Driven Subspaces for Large-Scale Optimization with Local Approximation Strategy, https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.08256.
    This work was mainly completed during my exchange study at UC Berkeley in Spring 2025. I would like to thank Dr Pengcheng Xie, who kindly offered me the opportunity to collaborate and provided me with precious advice as well as ideas during our collaboration. In this work, we designed a novel class of subspaces suitable for classical subspace-based optimization frameworks. Here is a poster about this work. The paper has been submitted to the Mathematics of Operations Research and is currently under review.
  • I am recently considering a problem of determining the anti-Ramsey number of $K_4^{(3)-}$ in complete 3-graphs.
